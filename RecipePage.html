How to make a Berries Smoothie

Berries for itself are a delicious way to obtain vitamins, energy, fructose and even collagen. For those mornings or afternoon were something cold is calling our apetite, a healthy option is a berries smoothie. So here it goes.

Ingredients

  • Oat milk
  • frozen blueberry
  • frozen strawberry
  • frozen raspberry
  • frozen blackberry

Instructions

  1. Take a blender or mixer and add 300 ml of oat milk
  2. Take one cup and a half of the froze berries and add it to the blender
  3. Add monkfruit at will
  4. Mix the ingredients until the fruit is combined and the consistency is medium-thick.
  5. If you prefer a thinner or more liquid consistency, add more milk to taste. For a healthier option, substitute the oat milk for water.
